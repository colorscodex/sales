Why Mediterranean Cuisine?

Because it is rich in vegetables, fruits, legumes, cereals (mainly whole grains), which together with the extra virgin olive oil, a hallmark of the Mediterranean Cuisine, produces very beneficial effects on cardiovascular disease, greater longevity and better functioning. of the brain, among many other benefits.[1]

And, all the recipes of Mediterranean cuisine are rich in flavors, aromas, colors and have been transmitted, in the homes of the area, from generation to generation. And best of all, they are simple when preparing them.
